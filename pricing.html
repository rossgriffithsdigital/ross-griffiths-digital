Common questions

Things people usually ask.

Do I own my website after it's built?

Yes, completely. Once your site is paid for and delivered, it's yours. You're not renting it from us.

Is the maintenance plan mandatory?

Not at all — it's entirely optional. Some clients prefer to manage their own site after launch, and that's completely fine. The maintenance plan is there if you'd rather not think about it.

Can I cancel maintenance if I change my mind?

Yes. Cancel any time with 30 days notice.

What if I need more than 5 pages?

Just get in touch and we'll put together a custom quote. Larger projects are priced based on scope — we'll always be upfront about the cost before any work begins.

How long does a build take?

Most sites are designed, built, and live within a couple of days. Timeline depends on how quickly we hear back from you on revisions — the faster you respond, the faster we move.

Not sure where to start?

Drop us a message and we'll figure it out together. No pressure, no commitment.
