Every project starts with a conversation.

Common questions

Things people usually ask.

What happens before you start building?

We kick off every project with a discovery session. That's a real conversation where we ask questions about your business, your customers, and your goals before we design anything. We never start until we understand what you actually need.

Do I own my website?

Yes, completely. You own the domain, the code, and all the content. We don't hold anything hostage.

Can I cancel my support plan?

Yes. Cancel any time with 30 days notice. No contracts, no penalties.

What if I need more than 5 pages?

Get in touch and we'll scope it out together. Larger projects are priced based on what's actually involved.

How long does a build take?

Most sites are designed, built, and live within 2 to 3 weeks of the discovery session.

Not sure where to start?

Drop us a message and we'll figure it out together. No pressure, no commitment.
