Our Story

Two People Who Care About This Place

Connor and Quinn have been best friends since they were four years old. They basically grew up in each other's houses. They went through school together, figured out a lot of things together, and now they run a business together.

They started Ross & Griffiths Digital because they kept seeing good businesses around Northumberland County lose customers to competitors with better websites. Not because those businesses were worse. Just because their online presence let them down.

They work on everything together on every project. There is no one person for design and one for the technical side. They do it all, side by side, the same way they have done everything since they were kids.
