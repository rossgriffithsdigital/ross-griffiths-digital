Who we are

Two friends. One goal.

R&GD is the result of years of friendship, shared skills, and a genuine belief that every local business deserves a proper presence online.

We've been friends for years — and frequent faces around Northumberland.

Connor and Quinn both grew up in Northumberland County. We've spent years as regulars at local shops, restaurants, and businesses across the area — places we genuinely care about and want to see thrive.

The idea for R&GD came naturally. We kept noticing great local businesses without a proper website — or with one that didn't reflect how good they actually were. Between our combined skills and a lot of time spent in this community, we knew we could help.

So we built a service around that. Every client works directly with us — no handoffs, no account managers, no runaround. Just two people who take the work seriously and care about getting it right.
